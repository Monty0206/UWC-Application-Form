How APS Works

Your APS (Admission Point Score) is calculated by adding up the points from your 6 best subjects, excluding Life Orientation. Each subject is rated from 1-7 based on your percentage:

  • 7 = 80-100% (Outstanding Achievement)
  • 6 = 70-79% (Meritorious Achievement)
  • 5 = 60-69% (Substantial Achievement)
  • 4 = 50-59% (Adequate Achievement)
  • 3 = 40-49% (Moderate Achievement)
  • 2 = 30-39% (Elementary Achievement)
  • 1 = 0-29% (Not Achieved)
